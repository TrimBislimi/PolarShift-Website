About

Polar Shift is a unique Swiss Metal band with a fresh sound that blends the best elements of Modern Metal with electronic elements, resulting in an infectious and captivating sound. Their introspective lyrics explore the ups and downs of relationships, personal growth, and finding one's place in the world. Lead singer Marcello Trusendi's vocals are supported by a dynamic instrumentation that ranges from dreamy synths to groovy guitar riffs and crushing breakdowns, creating a unique sound.

Polar Shift's music has been described as a fusion of modern Metalcore & Djent with elements of electronic and alternative, creating an unpredictable and exciting sonic experience. With their introspective and heartfelt lyrics, catchy choruses, and infectious rhythms, Polar Shift is a band that will leave their listeners feeling both emotionally moved and energized. Their newest singles "Defeatist" & "Inheritance", are a must-listen for fans of innovative and thought-provoking music.
